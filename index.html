Our Craft

The Art of Multan Blue Pottery

Multan has been the home of blue pottery — locally called kashi work — for over eight centuries. Our workshop in the old city continues this tradition using the same quartz-based paste, cobalt pigments, and hand-painting techniques passed down through generations.

Unlike ordinary clay pottery, Multan blue pottery is made from a unique blend of ground quartz, glass, and Multani mitti. Each piece is molded by hand, painted with copper oxide and cobalt blue, then fired at low temperature to create that signature brilliant blue glaze.

  • Workshop located in Multan, Punjab
  • Family-run business since 1987
  • Custom orders & bulk gifting available
  • Export-quality finishing
